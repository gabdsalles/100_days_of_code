The Best Movies According to Gabriel

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Parasite

Bong Joon-ho criticizes social inequality on Korea, telling the story of a family that start to work in the house of a very wealthy family, faking their identities.

Lost In Translation

It is kind of sad, but a very introspective movie, starring Scarlett Johansoon and Bill Murray, recorded in a Tokyo hotel.

The Perks of Being a Wallflower

The best coming-of-age movie i've ever seen, one of my favorite movie genres.
